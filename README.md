# Creatiq – Intellectual Property Rights Management Smart Contract

## Overview

Creatiq is a blockchain-based smart contract that enables creators to register, manage, and monetize their intellectual property rights. It provides a transparent, immutable, and automated framework for licensing, royalty distribution, and usage tracking, ensuring creators maintain control over their work while earning fair compensation.

## Key Features

* **Intellectual Property Registration**
  Securely record ownership details of creative works, patents, trademarks, or other intellectual property on-chain.
* **Transparent Licensing**
  Issue, manage, and revoke licenses with clearly defined usage terms visible to all stakeholders.
* **Automated Royalty Distribution**
  Distribute earnings automatically to rights holders based on predefined terms and agreements.
* **Usage Tracking**
  Track and verify usage events, ensuring compliance with license terms and identifying unauthorized use.
* **Immutable Proof of Ownership**
  Maintain a permanent and verifiable record of intellectual property rights without reliance on intermediaries.

## Contract Components

1. **Registration Module** – Records intellectual property details including creator information, title, and proof of originality.
2. **Licensing Module** – Manages creation, validation, and termination of usage licenses.
3. **Royalty Module** – Automates calculation and payment of royalties to registered beneficiaries.
4. **Tracking Module** – Monitors and logs content usage events for transparency.
5. **Access Control** – Ensures only authorized parties can modify or update specific contract data.

## Workflow

1. **Registration** – The creator submits IP details to the blockchain for verification and storage.
2. **Licensing** – The creator or rights holder issues licenses to authorized users or organizations.
3. **Usage Logging** – The contract records usage events tied to licensed works.
4. **Royalty Distribution** – Earnings are automatically split and sent to the rightful beneficiaries.

## Benefits

* Enhances trust between creators and consumers
* Removes intermediaries, reducing costs and delays
* Prevents unauthorized use through transparent verification
* Supports global monetization opportunities

## Example Use Cases

* Musicians registering songs and receiving automated royalties from streaming platforms
* Authors licensing eBooks to digital distributors
* Inventors securing patents and selling usage rights to manufacturers
* Artists tracking NFT art usage and ensuring fair payment
